Seas The Day

Seas(Seize) the Day, get it? Seize and seas? Play on words..? Errmm.. anyways.. Seas the Day was my capstone project for Actualize where I personally developed my very own app! The functionality of this application is to make it easier for the everyday traveler to Japan to enjoy their time on their vacation rather than worry about planning it. How it works, is simple. When a user, or as I like to call it, travler, visits the app, they're presented with a variety of activities to view from and choose to their liking. If a traveler has an account, they can add certain activities and build up their own itineray list of their liking! If you'd like a to see what I'm talking about more in depth, check out my presentation video! In the coming days, I'm hoping to make it the best possible version of it yet!
